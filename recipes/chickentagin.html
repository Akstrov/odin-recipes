Chicken Tagine

This Moroccan chicken tagine recipe pairs sweet and savory stewed chicken with dried fruits and exotic spices. Children love the familiarity of sweet spices and dried fruits. Serve over couscous and garnish with toasted almonds and fresh cilantro.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup chopped dried apricots
  • ¼ cup raisins
  • 4 bone-in chicken thighs
  • ¼ cup olive oil, or as needed
  • ½ small onion, minced
  • 2 teaspoons chopped garlic
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • ¼ cup honey
  • ½ bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
  • ¼ cup toasted sliced almonds
